Where to begin?

  1. Download Super CSV
  2. Choose a reader or writer - there are 4 of each to choose from!
  3. Choose your preferences for reading/writing (including choice of delimiter, quote character, and end of line symbols)
  4. Choose from a wide variety of cell processors to apply conversions or constraints to your data
  5. Check out the reading and writing examples to see how it's all put together!
